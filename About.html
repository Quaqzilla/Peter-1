Not The Bored Room Strategic Story Telling

Not The Bored Room is the strategic storytelling consultancy and storytelling skills training company
established by Peter Christie, aka Big Chief Talking Bull, in 1990. Peter can fairly claim to have helped
pioneer the storytelling in organisations movement in the world, having now amassed 35 years of practical
experience in the field.

Big Chief Talking Bull qualified with an MA in Industrial Psychology degree from Wits University, after
which he worked in the corporate sector for five years, with Ford Motor Company, the Chamber of Mines
and Liberty Life. Recognising the problems of poor communication besetting many organisations, he initiated
Not the Bored Room. The company’s work focuses primarily on strategic storytelling for creative corporate
communications, marketing and sales, change and transformation, and leadership development purposes.

Not the Bored Room provides three core storytelling services:

  • Strategic Storytelling Consulting
  • Strategic Storybooks
  • Strategic Skills Training Workshops and Coaching

    Strategic Storybooks

    Not the Bored Room engages with clients who are celebrating and commemorating key milestones in their life histories, such
    as anniversaries, the achievement of major accomplishments, a merger or an acquisition, the retirement of a leaders of
    leaders, and such like.

    We then manage the research, writing, editing and production of a strategic storybook based on the client’s specific requirements.

    The writing aspect of the storybooks is undertaken by Peter Christie himself. Peter has authored and edited numerous books for
    the public, including lead editing (with Ronnie Lessem and Lovemore Mbigi) the best-selling African Management: Philosophies,
    Concepts and Applications (1993), and authoring Stories from an Afman! (1996), Animal Firm (2000) with Craig Gordon, Every Leader
    a Storyteller – Breathing Brightness into Business (2009), The Treasure Punt (2013), Inclusive People (2017), and The Charlatans
    (2019). He has also published several articles in local and international journals, in both the academic and popular press, and
    wrote a series of 122 short story newspaper columns for over a decade.

    Peter has authored numerous strategic storybooks, the most recent being Building Better Lives Together – The story of HISP,
    a rambunctious teenager and its tumultuous transition to adulthood (2022).

    Storytelling Skills Training and Coaching

    Peter Christie has lead facilitated seven International Storytelling in Organisations workshops, at Emerson College and Hawkwood
    College in England, and in various venues in South Africa, and the Personal Leadership and Storytelling module for MBA students
    at City University, London. As a part-time lecturer at Wits Business School for 31 years, he developed and facilitated the highly-
    rated and popular Every Leader a Storyteller MBA elective, after having co-ordinated the South African Management Project at WBS
    in the early 1990s, a project investigating indigenous approaches to leadership and management in the African context.
